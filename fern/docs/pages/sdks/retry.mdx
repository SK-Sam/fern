By default, Fern employs an exponential backoff strategy with a maximum of two retries for each client. Users of the SDK can easily customize this behavior by adjusting the `maxRetries` parameter within the `config` section of the `generators.yml` file. Below is an illustrative example:

<CodeBlock title="generators.yml">
  ```mdx
  groups:
  branch-python:
    generators:
      - name: fernapi/fern-python-sdk
        version: 0.6.5
        github:
          repository: some-staging-repository
          mode: pull-request
        config:
          maxRetries: 6
</CodeBlock>
